Andrei Tarkovsky

Filmmaker

1932-1986

"For me the most
interesting characters
are outwardly static,
but inwardly charged
by an overriding
passion."

Andrei Arsenyevich
Tarkovsky was a
Russian filmmaker,
writer, film editor,
film theorist,
theatre and opera
director.

Tarkovsky's works Andrei Rublev,
Solaris, Mirror, and Stalker are
regularly listed among the greatest
films of all time.

His contribution to
cinema was so
influential that
works done in a
similar way are
described as
Tarkovskian

His work is characterized by long takes,
unconventional dramatic structure,
distinctly authored use of cinematography,
and spiritual & metaphysical themes.
